About Us

As college students, we have designed Alcojolla for users to educate themselves and others about the dangers that alcohol puts them in. Because at the end of the day, YOU control whether or not you drink. We cannot make that decision for you. Instead, our application caters to the knowledge of its users, by giving them the facts and risks unique to each individual user's personal input.
